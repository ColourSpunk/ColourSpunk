How To Get To Lusty Beg Island

Lusty Beg Island is approximately a 2 hour drive from Bangor, Co. Down and around 1 hour and 40 minutes from Belfast. The Island is accessed by a small, free car ferry which departs regularly from Boa Island outside Kesh.

Car parking is available outside your accommodation on the island or in the ferry car park on Boa Island.

You may be asked to leave your car on Boa Island until you check in to your accommodation.

The ferry will continue running until the end of the night for guests not staying on the island.
