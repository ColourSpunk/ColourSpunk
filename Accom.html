4* Lodges and Chalets

All our houses overlook the lake and enjoy a secluded position amongst mature woodland. All include a fully fitted kitchen with washing machine, dishwasher, fridge, oven and microwave. There is a living/dining area & verandah.

Houses sleeping six

One Double, one twin and bathroom with bath upstairs and twin and bathroom with shower downstairs. There is also an upstairs verandahh and wood burning stove with starter pack provided.

Houses sleeping five

One double with en suite, one twin and one single with family bathroom with shower.

Houses sleeping four

One double and one twin and bathroom with shower.

Houses sleeping two

One double with en suite with shower and bath and wood burning stove with starter pack included.

Open plan living, each with a double bed, kitchenette (perfect for preparing snacks or keeping wine chilled) and their own private verandah. Each cabin includes a few extras above and beyond the expected such as a wine cooler, microwave, toaster and even a kitchen sink! No oven or hob though.
These rooms come in single, double, twin, triple and family occupancy. Each room includes a TV, iron and board, hairdryer, study desk and tea/coffee making facilities.
To book in advance with Lusty Beg you will need phone reception on 028 686 33300 stating that you are a guest of the Wilson-Warke Wedding. Accommodation booking will be released on www.lustybegisland.com a year before the wedding, but as our date falls on the ‘Half Term’ week for schools we anticipate that this might fill up quickly!
