On the day...

Please plan to arrive an hour before the ceremony to allow plenty of time for the ferry crossing over to Lusty Beg Island. It is only a two minute crossing but the ferry can only accommodate two cars and a certain amount of foot passengers at a time so there may be a wait! We plan to have a member of the wedding party to guide you from the car park on Boa Island.

If you are checking into accommodation on the island on the day of the wedding, your room may not be ready until after the ceremony. You can leave any luggage in your car as there will be plenty of time to check in during the drinks reception.

Weather permitting, we are hoping to have an outdoor ceremony so please dress accordingly for October temperatures!
